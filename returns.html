Our Return Promise

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a simple and hassle-free return process.

30-Day Returns

Return items within 30 days of delivery

Free Returns

No cost for returns on eligible items

Easy Exchange

Quick process for size or color swaps

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • In the original packaging
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Personal care items
  • Intimate apparel
  • Customized products
  • Digital downloads
  • Gift cards
  • Clearance items (marked as final sale)

Return Process

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and select the items you wish to return

2

Print Label

Download and print the prepaid return shipping label

3

Pack Items

Securely pack items in original packaging

4

Ship

Drop off package at any authorized shipping location

Refund Process

Once we receive your return:

  • We'll inspect the item within 2 business days
  • You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed
  • Refund will be issued to your original payment method
  • Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account

Exchanges

For exchanges:

  • Select "Exchange" instead of "Return" when initiating the process
  • Choose the new size or color you want
  • We'll ship the new item once we receive your return
  • Exchanges are subject to item availability

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage
  • We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund
  • Return shipping will be covered by us
