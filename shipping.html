Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer.

  • Orders placed before 2 PM EST on business days are processed the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM EST are processed the next business day
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day

Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to the following locations:

  • All 50 United States and U.S. territories
  • Canada
  • Select international destinations

Note: International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

Free Shipping

Free standard shipping is available on orders over $50 within the continental United States. This offer:

  • Applies to standard shipping only
  • Excludes oversized items
  • Cannot be combined with other shipping promotions

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive:

  • A shipping confirmation email with tracking information
  • Access to real-time tracking through your account dashboard
  • SMS updates if opted in for text notifications

Shipping Restrictions

Some restrictions apply to certain items:

  • Hazardous materials cannot be shipped via air
  • Oversized items may require additional shipping charges
  • Some items cannot be shipped to international destinations

Lost or Damaged Packages

In the event of a lost or damaged package:

  • Contact our customer service within 5 business days of the expected delivery date
  • Provide order number and photos of damaged items if applicable
  • We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly
